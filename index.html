Cross-Stitch Pixel Art Maker

Choose the size of your fabric (no bigger than 40x40, or click 'Submit' for a pre-set size), choose your colors, and click around to create a work of art!

Change the fabric color at any time.

To change the color of a stitch, just choose a new thread color and click on the stitch. Click a second time to remove a stitch.
